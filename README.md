# Mandalina
Mandalina uniquely blends the authenticity of traditional Egyptian desserts with innovative, fruit-inspired creations that are lighter, fresher, and crafted with premium natural ingredients - offering indulgence with balance.*

## project idea

At Mandalina, we believe dessert should make you feel good — not slow you down.
Our sweets are made fresh every day, with ingredients picked for their flavor, not just for show. We focus on light textures, real taste, and simple combinations that work — no fuss, just feel-good food.

What makes us different isn’t just what we put in, but how we think about dessert. It’s not a reward or a cheat — it’s part of your day, and it should feel right.
Whether you’re on a quick break, out and about, or just need something that hits the spot without hitting too hard — we’ve got you.

we believe in:
	•	Fresh, daily-made desserts that actually taste fresh
	•	Simple ingredients with real flavor
	•	Light textures that don’t weigh you down
	•	Quick, satisfying moments that fit into your day

## work plan
  1. Research & Analysis
  
Audience Personas: Define target groups (dessert lovers, health-conscious customers, families, youth, etc.).
Competitor Analysis: Study competitors in local and global markets for desserts and healthy snacks.
Moodboard: Collect images, colors, designs, fruits, and desserts to inspire the brand’s visual style.
Visual Identity Inspiration: Examples of dessert and healthy snack branding, photography styles, and graphic patterns.

2. Visual Identity

Logo Design: Create a logo that combines fun, freshness, and the healthy aspect.
Color Palette & Typography: Choose colors that represent sweetness and nature and retro, readable fonts.
Style Guide: Guidelines for using the logo, colors, typography, and applying the brand identity across products.

3. Main Designs

Packaging & Poster: Design packaging for desserts and fruit products, and promotional posters.
Tri-fold Brochure: Brochure showcasing products and their health benefits.
Social Media Carousel: Instagram/Facebook carousel posts highlighting products attractively.
Merchandising: Design T-shirts, mugs, and tote bags featuring the brand’s visual elements.


4. Review & Finalization

Team Review & Improvements: Evaluate all designs and make final improvements.
Export & Delivery Specs: Prepare files for print and digital use.
Final Presentation:
Behance-style Portfolio: Comprehensive showcase of the brand and designs.
Presentation PDF: Ready-to-present file for clients or team.
Upload to GitHub: Organize and store all files and documentation.
